Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Draft Kings (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you visit our website, take part in campaigns and promotions, or use our related services (the “Services”).

1. Information we collect

  • Account & contact data: name, email, phone, country/region, age confirmation.
  • Transactional data: deposits/withdrawals (if applicable), reward credits, referral redemptions.
  • Usage & device data: IP address, device identifiers, browser type, pages viewed, timestamps, referral URLs, campaign codes.
  • Cookies & similar tech: cookies, web beacons, local storage used for login, analytics, fraud prevention, and personalization.
  • Support & communications: messages you send us and related metadata.

2. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Operate Campaigns (eligibility checks, credits, leaderboards, anti-fraud).
  • Personalize content, remember preferences, and recommend offers.
  • Process payments and withdrawals (if applicable).
  • Communicate with you about updates, Campaigns, changes to terms, and security notices.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. Legal bases for processing (EEA/UK only)

  • Contract: to provide the Services you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure, improve, and market our Services (balanced against your rights).
  • Consent: for certain cookies/marketing where required. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation: to meet compliance and record-keeping duties.

4. Sharing of information

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, payments, anti-fraud, customer support—under contracts requiring confidentiality and security.
  • Legal & compliance: to comply with laws, lawful requests, or to protect users and our Services.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, as permitted by law.

5. Cookies and tracking

We use necessary cookies for sign-in and site security, and optional cookies for analytics and marketing. You can manage preferences via your browser or the cookie banner (where provided). Blocking cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Services, for legitimate business needs (e.g., records, fraud prevention), and to meet legal obligations. When no longer required, we take steps to delete or anonymize data.

7. Your rights

  • Access, correction, deletion: request a copy, fix inaccuracies, or ask to delete where applicable.
  • Opt out of marketing: use the unsubscribe link or contact us.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): you may request access, deletion, and correction of personal information; we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising without consent.
  • GDPR (EEA/UK): rights include access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

8. Security

We use technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk to help protect your information. No system is 100% secure; please use strong passwords and keep credentials confidential.

9. Children’s privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us to remove it.

10. International transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country. Where required, we use safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

11. Third-party sites & social

Our Services may link to third-party websites or social features. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. We will post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date. Material changes may be notified via email or in-product notice.

13. Contact

To exercise your rights or ask questions: privacy@glideralive.site.
